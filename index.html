About UMIC

Unmesh Mashruwala Innovation Cell, also widely known as UMIC, is a tech team formed in the late 90s to introduce the field of robotics to students. The team consists of 80+ diligent and dedicated students from IIT Bombay working towards embracing and developing the idea of an autonomous future. The team at Unmesh Mashruwala Innovation Cell is comprised of experts in various disciplines such as robotics, artificial intelligence, control systems, computer science, and electrical engineering. UMIC works on the principle of co-existence of machines and humans developing automated prototypes to make the world a safer and more sustainable place. Under the sheds of UMIC, the primary area of focus is developing autonomous vehicles.

  • We were founded in early 2000s and the team is named after Unmesh Mashruwala, an alumnus at IIT Bombay
  • We are the oldest technical team at IIT Bombay and have received several accolades for our contributions to the world of autonomy
  • We have collaborated with companies and NGOs like Kudan and WWF to provide software solutions
